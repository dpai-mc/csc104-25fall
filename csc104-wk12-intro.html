CSC104 - Week 12: Introduction to PowerPoint

Aloha, and welcome to Week 12! This week we begin our exploration of PowerPoint, Microsoft's presentation software. We'll focus on the fundamentals: creating and editing presentations, working with themes, adding and organizing slides, incorporating images, and understanding the PowerPoint interface. PowerPoint tends to be more intuitive than Excel, as many of the formatting concepts carry over from Word. The key is learning how to organize information visually on slides and navigate PowerPoint's unique layout tools.
