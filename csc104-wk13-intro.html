CSC104 - Week 13: Enhancing PowerPoint with Shapes and SmartArt

Aloha, and welcome to Week 13! This is a short week for Thanksgiving, but we're continuing our PowerPoint exploration. This week focuses on enhancing your presentations using shapes and SmartArt. These tools help you create more engaging, visually appealing slides that effectively communicate your ideas. We'll explore how to add shapes, arrange them effectively, and use SmartArt to represent relationships and processes visually. By the end of this week, you'll be able to transform basic slides into more polished and professional presentations.
